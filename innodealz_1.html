Refund & Cancellation Policy +

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Because our work is custom professional services, refunds are handled as follows.

Deposits & milestones

Once work has begun, deposits are generally non-refundable as they cover committed time and resources. For milestone-based projects, payment for a delivered and approved milestone is non-refundable; future milestones not yet started may be cancelled.

Cancellation by you

You may cancel anytime in writing to info@innodealz.com. You'll be billed for work completed to date; amounts paid for work not yet started are refunded, minus non-recoverable third-party costs and payment-processing fees.

Eligible for refund

  • Deposit paid but no work started.
  • Payment for a milestone not yet started.
  • Duplicate or incorrect payments.

Refunds are generally not available for work already delivered, change-of-mind after delivery, or delays caused by missing client materials/approvals.

How to request

Email your name, project details, payment date, and reason. We review requests within 5 business days and issue approved refunds to the original payment method.
